### MCSS Announcement: The Show

<p style="text-align: left; font-size:70%;">
The Show is a 6 hour extravaganza dedicated to you, the MCSS student
community, a night of great food, fun, and freebies! It'll be a night
you won't forget! Come relax and unwind, there'll be plenty of fun
games and activities going on throughout the night. They’ll be a
video game station (with PC, XBox, PS4 and Wii games), arts and
crafts, and board games. They'll even be competitions such as our AI
competition and Capture the flag. They'll even be a scavenger hunt!
With every competition, you win you’ll be entered in a raffle draw
for all the freebies we have to giveaway! There will also be plenty
of workshops, our SPRINT and Flutter workshops will be hosted by
google engineers themselves! These aren’t even all the activities
and events we have lined up! Each week we'll be releasing more and
more information about The Show. For information about all events
announced thus far check out the Eventbrite link, and make sure to
register.</p>
